About Day Hop

Can you remember your life? I can't, that's why I made Day Hop! An app that lets you write a tweet-sized post about what you did on a particular day. Give it a title, give it a body, (change the date if you forgot to post yesterday) and you're all set.


BUT WAIT! THERE IS MORE! You can also see what post you made on any day a year ago... and the year before that... and the year before that... etc.
